Hosting studies on Discoveries Online

We have currently paused hosting studies for other labs on Discoveries Online.

As an alternative, you can run Qualtrics studies with webcam functionality on the Lookit platform. Click here to find their guide for using their platform.

Internal studies on Lookit

In order to use Lookit’s webcam functionality, you will need to create an ‘internal’ study. This means that you will use their experiment runner to set up your study, even if you have already created a Qualtrics survey.

Embedding your Qualtrics link

In order to embed your Qualtrics link into an internal Lookit study you will need to utilize the “iframe” frame when setting up the protocol configuration for the study on Lookit. Click here for more information and examples.

Join their Slack workspace for assistance with any questions or technical issues!
